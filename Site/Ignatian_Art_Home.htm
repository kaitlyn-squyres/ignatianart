Invitation to Ignatian Art

By: Kaitlyn Squyres

"Invitation to Ignatian Art" is an art journal for those looking to practice Ignatian Spirituality through creative means. It begins with an introduction to Ignatian Spirituality and why the arts are important to this imaginative tradition and includes notes on how to use the guided journal. The main content of the workbook is based on the four weeks of the original Spiritual Exercises, with each "week" or section beginning with a quote from a spiritual figure, an overview of that week, and a multi-step creative prompt. The goal of this guided art journal is to make a text-based tradition more accessible to creative people of all abilities.

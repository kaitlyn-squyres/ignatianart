Kaitlyn Squyres

Hello! I am a creative working in New York City who is passionate about the connection between the arts and spirituality. My primary mediums are graphic design and photography. I currently serve as the Head Graphics Editor for The Observer, the student newspaper of Fordham University's Lincoln Center campus, and I love creating graphics both for specific articles and to enhance the overall feeling of the print issues. In my photography, I love to capture the spirit or story of a place.

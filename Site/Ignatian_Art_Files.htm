The Examen

The Examen consists of five points meant to prayerfully review and reflect on how one's day has been. These creative prompts will help you visualize how your day has been in relation to how you spend your time and where you spend your day.

Because these exercises are repetitive, I recommend only doing one per day. The examen prompts and visual guides are only provided once in the journal, but they can be downloaded here as pdfs.
